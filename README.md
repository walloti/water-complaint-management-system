# Water Complaint Management System

## Project Description

The Water Complaint Management System is designed to streamline the process of reporting and managing water-related complaints from citizens to the concerned authorities. The system allows users to submit complaints about water quality, supply issues, and other related problems. It enables efficient tracking, management, and resolution of complaints, thereby enhancing the communication between citizens and the water supply management entities.

## Features
- User-friendly interface for easy complaint submission.
- Complaint tracking and status updates.
- Notifications to users regarding complaint progress.
- Reporting and analytics for authorities to understand common issues and areas for improvement.
